I am currently a Postdoctoral fellow in the \MYhref[awesome]{http://www.psy.cmu.edu/~coaxlab/}{\textbf{CoAx Lab}} at Carnegie Mellon University (PI: Dr. Timothy Verstynen). My research focuses on synthesizing \MYhref[awesome]{http://www.psy.cmu.edu/~coaxlab/documents/Dunovan_Verstynen_2016.pdf}{\textbf{cognitive algorithms}} with biologically detailed \MYhref[awesome]{https://www.biorxiv.org/content/biorxiv/early/2018/10/07/418756.full.pdf}{\textbf{neural networks}} to capture a more complete understanding of intelligent behavior. Seeing the benefits of the interdisciplinary approach, my recent efforts have turned towards embedding neural and cognitive dynamics in artificial agents to explore solutions to creating \MYhref[awesome]{https://arxiv.org/pdf/1809.09147.pdf}{\textbf{safer}} \textbf{and} \MYhref[awesome]{https://arxiv.org/pdf/1809.03406.pdf}{\textbf{more flexible}} \textbf{AI}. Moving forward, I am seeking positions (in academia or industry) where I can bring my knowledge and expertise to bear on interesting and important questions while continuing to learn and grow as a researcher and scientist.
